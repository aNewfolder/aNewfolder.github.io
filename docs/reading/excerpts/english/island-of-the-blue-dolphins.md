# Island of the Blue Dolphins

Sadly, she was never able to tell the story herself, for her language was one that no one could comprehend. So the details were **pieced together, guessed at, fabricated, romanticized at times, embroidered in the retellings.**

**fabricate** 捏造、组装

**embroider** 刺绣、加以渲染

I sang as I went down the trail to the beach and along the beach to the sandspit. **I felt that the day was an omen of good fortune.**

He **trotted** past me and up the **ravine**. When I got to the house he was there waiting, as if he had not been away or nothing had happened.

In all the time he lived, Rontu never left again, and the wild dogs, which for some reason divided into two packs, after that never returned to the **headland**.

**trot** 小跑

**ravine** 峡谷

**headland** 岬

After the eggs hatched, I put shreds of **abalone** under the tree and these she fed her young.

**abalone** 鲍鱼

These I bound together with **sinew** and covered with female **kelp**, which has broad leaves.

**sinew** 筋，肌腱

**kelp** 海藻

For a long time I stood and looked back at the Island of the Blue Dolphins. The last thing I saw of it was the high headland. I thought of Rontu lying there beneath the stones of many colors, and of Won-a-nee, wherever she was, and the little red fox that would scratch in vain at my fence, and my canoe hidden in the cave, and of all the happy days.

Dolphins rose out of the sea and swam before the ship. The swam for many **leagues** in the morning through the bright water, weaving their foamy patterns. The little birds were chirping in their cage and Rontu-Aru sat beside me.

**league** 里格（约5km）



关于自然，字里行间夹杂着《海鸥乔纳森》和《野性的呼唤》的影子。《海鸥乔纳森》在用很强的代入感和虽然简单却仍然很有氛围感的遣词造句吸引我，《野性的呼唤》则更多有一种冷峻的第一视角描述，加上别出心裁的用词，词汇量也不算小，总体很符合社会达尔文主义那种弱肉强食的意境。但这本《蓝色的海豚岛》，关于专业知识的用词并不克制，甚至有一点点《远离尘嚣》式的大段描写的感觉，但在真正氛围的顶点，往往需要引出精彩绝伦的句子的时候，笔下又极其克制，用非常简短，朴素的句子去承接，就像《一步之遥》里左手砸下整个键盘上最低的一个八度do，也像一拳打在了地上，似乎什么都没打中，却又有种很沉郁的深度。

整体还是颇为冷峻的第一人称有限视角，像《第七天》中毫无感情的描述。理所当然地trim掉小鸟的羽毛，把药撒到spring里诱使小狗喝水睡着后抱走栓到院子里，即便是为了make friends，即便是与father相比已经显露出深厚的sympathy，但依旧不知道在褒扬还是暗含批判。

很多很怪异的名字，Rontu，Won-a-nee，两只小鸟，结尾两段更像一种朴素的对动物朋友们的罗列，大概用不了几天就会忘掉每一个名字对应着谁。结尾一句 **and of all the happy days.** 过于朴素的描述，依旧抓着这一点不放，带来的并未小说结尾那种朦胧却又淹没一切的对ending的感伤，更像是一段理性的热恋后清晰而收敛的回响。
